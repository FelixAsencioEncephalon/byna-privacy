Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

Byna ("the app") is a binaural beats and meditation app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: none).

Data Collection

Byna does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data — including your settings, session history, and purchase status — is stored locally on your device only.

Accounts

Byna does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or authentication of any kind.

Analytics & Tracking

Byna does not use any analytics services, crash reporters, or tracking SDKs. No usage data leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

Byna offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. This transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Byna does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Byna does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
